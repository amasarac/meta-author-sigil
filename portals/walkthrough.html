Guided Walkthrough — Cognitive Glass

Follow these steps in a quiet room with headphones for best results.

1) Open the Resonance Mapper

Open lexeme_tone_mapper.html in a new tab. Inspect the nodes along the log-frequency axis.

2) Auto-load eigen_map.json

The mapper will have nodes populated from eigen_map.json. If not, click the load/import control in the mapper.

3) Initialize Audio

In both pages, click Initialize Audio or play a node — your browser may prompt for audio/mic permissions.

4) Run a Sweep

In the mapper, set a comfortable start/end and press Start Sweep. Breathe with the pulse and note any resonant sensations.

5) Use Mic Peak Detection

On the player page, click Enable Mic then Start Detection. Hum or sing as the mapper plays tones to help the detector pick peaks. Press Apply Marks to push the detected peaks into the mapper context.

6) Save & Iterate

Export detected marks from the player and import into the mapper as needed. Tweak thresholds for your environment.

Tip: Use a stable tone source (phone tone generator) and quiet room for best detection. Enjoy responsibly.
